⚠️ Before you use Frankfurt Radar

Frankfurt Radar is an unofficial, open-source community tool for Frankfurt's English-speaking residents. It automatically fetches and translates public alerts from:

RMV (transit) · DWD via BrightSky (weather) · Polizei Frankfurt (police notices) · Autobahn GmbH (motorway disruptions) · Stadt Frankfurt (city road works) · Visit Frankfurt (festivals) · OpenLigaDB (Bundesliga) · Ticketmaster (sports events) · Wikimedia Commons (event photos, CC-licensed)

This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or monitored by any of these organisations. Data may be delayed or incomplete.

Do not rely on this site in an emergency. Always contact 112 (emergency) or 110 (police) directly, and refer to official sources for authoritative information.
